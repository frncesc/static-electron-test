To create a picture, enter editing mode. Press W and select the picture widget from a list. Then click on the widget to upload an image.

 

 

 

 

Put the widget here ↓

You can also drag and drop a picture straight from your desktop or use our library, powered by Unsplash and Flickr. GIFs are supported as well.

Drag and drop ↓

Now it's time to make things fancier. Change the radius of the picture widget, making the corners more or less rounded, or set a border.

Another important feature is the crop.

Double click on image in editing mode to see what's going on here.

After you’ve selected a crop tool, use the scroll bar to scale the picture or customize the crop box manually.

If you don't like the result, undo shortcut works here, as it does nearly everywhere within Readymag.
